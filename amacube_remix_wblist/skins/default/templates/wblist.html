Sender / Domain Policy

You may define rules that will accept or reject email from certain senders or domain. These rules are evaluted by most specific match. Once a Rule is matched, no further rules are evaluated. It is important to consider the order of evaluation when creating rules.

Example: Deny all email from example.org except for email sent by Bob (2 rules):
(Rule 1) Sender: Bob@example.org, Policy: Whitelist
(Rule 2) Sender: @example.org, Policy: Blacklist

Order of Evaluation:
user@sub.example.org
user
@sub.example.org
@example.org
@org
@. (This is a catchall, meaning it matches everything)
